Information Technology Student Association

Sam Houston State University

The Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) at Sam Houston State University welcomes those who are interested in the IT field in hopes to further their knowledge beyond the scope of the classroom. ITSA @ SHSU is dedicated to helping students open doors to their future careers with first-hand internship and job opportunities, networking by creating a closer sense of community on campus, and providing resources for self-development.

We strive to help our members be equipped with the best foundational structure in order to succeed and give them the tools they need to go into the workplace.

What We Do

At ITSA, we bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world IT experience. Our members engage in hands-on projects, workshops, and networking events designed to enhance their technical skills and professional development. We collaborate with industry professionals to bring guest speakers, host coding bootcamps, and organize hackathons that challenge our members to solve real-world problems using cutting-edge technology.

Beyond technical training, we foster a tight-knit community where students can connect with peers who share their passion for IT. From cybersecurity seminars to software development sprints, ITSA provides opportunities to explore diverse areas of information technology while building a portfolio of work that stands out to future employers. Join us to gain practical experience, expand your network, and prepare for a successful career in the ever-evolving tech industry.
